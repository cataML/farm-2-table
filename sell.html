Frequently Asked Questions

What can I sell?
You can sell a wide variety of farm-fresh goods—including fruits, vegetables, dairy, grains, meats, and more. If it’s natural and grown or made on your farm, it belongs here.
Are there any fees?
No upfront or listing fees! We only charge a small commission on each sale, so you earn more with every successful order.
How do I get paid?
Payouts are fast and secure via M-Pesa, PayPal, or direct bank transfer. Choose what works best for you.
How do I place an order?
Add products to your cart, fill out your shipping info, and complete payment at checkout.
What payment methods are accepted?
We support M-Pesa, PayPal, and bank transfers for your convenience.
Can I cancel or change my order?
Yes, contact our support team within 2 hours to change or cancel your order.
